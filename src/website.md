📖 FOUNDATIONAL PHILOSOPHY
Content Voice & Tone Principles:
1. Speak Human, Not Corporate:
❌ "Leverage our synergistic AI solutions to optimize operational efficiency"
✅ "Your teams are drowning in manual work. We get it. Let's fix that together."
2. Lead with Empathy, Follow with Evidence:
Start every page acknowledging the pain
Then show the path forward
End with proof it works
3. Conversational, Not Salesy:
Write like you're having coffee with a tired COO at 3 PM
Use contractions (you're, we'll, it's)
Ask rhetorical questions
Address the reader as "you"
Be honest about challenges
4. Inclusive & Accessible:
Avoid jargon unless necessary (then explain it)
Short sentences (12-15 words average)
Break up text with subheadings every 2-3 paragraphs
Use examples anyone can understand
5. Psychology-Driven:
Address fears before benefits
Use social proof strategically
Create urgency without pressure
Build trust through transparency
